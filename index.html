NeoFreeBird

A modified Twitter app, with branding reverts, BHTwitter and other QoL modifications.

"So stylish
Baby X's all gone"

NeoFreeBird reverts the new rebrand, so you can say hello to your favorite bird again.

Ads? What's that?

Block Twitter ads and get access to multiple Blue features for free using the modified BHTwitter version on NeoFreeBird, like Undo Tweet, app icons, themes and much much more!

Twitter, Twitter for...iPhone?

NeoFreeBird returns the beloved Tweet source labels, so you know what client people use to Tweet. How about them apples, jacksfilms?

NeoFreeBird speaks your language. Properly.

All of Twitters localizations are included and somewhat improved, so it won't matter if you speak English, Swedish or Japanese, NeoFreeBird will speak your language!

Want to build it yourself? Go ahead!

If you prefer to build your own version of NeoFreeBird with your own Twitter IPA, we have guides on how to do just that!
