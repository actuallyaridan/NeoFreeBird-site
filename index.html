NeoFreeBird

A modified Twitter app, with branding reverts, BHTwitter and similar.

"So stylish
Baby X's all gone"

NeoFreeBird reverts the new rebrand, so you can say hello to your favoutite bird again.

Ads? What's that?

Customize Twitter using the included version of BHTwitter in order to block ads, change the app icon, pick a theme and gain access to most Twitter Blue features -- all for free

NeoFreeBird is a fork of nyathea's FreeBird and is developed by Adnan Bukvic and @timi2506
